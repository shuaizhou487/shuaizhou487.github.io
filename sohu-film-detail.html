SoHu Film Website Design

A HD-quality movies paid website

BACKGROUND


According to the online survey, more than 28.7% users (200 thousand users completed our online survey in total) want a professional online movies product. Also the online survey data shows the users have certain spending power and expense intention.

RESEARCH

How could we design more better than other simialr products? The most of these products provide the similar contents. How could we stand out from the competition? In this phase, we did the online survey to gather the user behavior and preference, then we did the user interview to find the user requirements. In doing so, we mapped out the user journey try to find the key problems, lead us to design a better product.

IDEATION

Our design idea presented a distinctive interface design style and focus on the detail interaction design, incredibly reduce the users' operation time and help users to search movies more efficiency, also made their browse routines more personalized and joyful.

Users can get on-click access to login and payment by scan the QR code. Users can filter movies by years, type, company, hit, popular and search bar.
