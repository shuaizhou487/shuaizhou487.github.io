TourConnect

BACKGROUND

TourConnect is an online, global community for the B2B tourism/travel industry. There has two main services from TourConnect. First, TourConnect can help you find new business partners or stay connected with your current partners in our network. Second, TourConnect have a tool to help you build and manage the e-contracts.

I was joined TourConnect as the first designer. My work include new features development and redesign the existing product to improve a better experience for user. I focus on streamlines product workflow and design doable solutions. I was responsible for whole design process including research, use-case analysis, user feedback review, user flow design, prototyping and user interface design.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Redesign the current TourConnect website product in an attempt to increase usage and improve ease of use.

ANALYSIS

Problems

I was redesign the search page at the beginning because of I thought the main problem was the bad experience on search partner page.Through the research I found the problem was not only caused by search partner page. So I was changed my original idea.

IDEATION

After rounds of ideation and refine, our final design divided our target users into five categorise, and customized the new user flows related their scenarios and primary goals. More specifically, we redefined the user categories and identified users' goals at sign up，regrouped informations and guided them by bubble box to complete their company profile in order to accomplish their primary goal. We also cooperated with trigger email to encourage users to make their profile more pretty. The new user flows incredibly reduced the information input time and operation steps. Additional, they enhanced the product usability to make users don’t need to think about how to use our product.
